About Mamma Ngone

Bringing the Heart of Douala to Your Table.

Based in the vibrant city of Douala, Mamma Ngone has dedicated her life to unearthing the most authentic, natural treasures of Cameroon. What started as a small local stall has blossomed into a bridge connecting the rich soils of our homeland with the global diaspora.

We believe in purity. Our forest honey is raw and unpasteurized, our Penja pepper is globally renowned, and our coffee is roasted to capture the morning spirit of the highlands. Whether you are a local resident or miles away from home, we deliver a taste of nostalgia and wellness directly to you.
